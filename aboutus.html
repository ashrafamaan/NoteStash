About us

Who are we?

Welcome to NoteStash—your ultimate platform for sharing and accessing university notes! Whether you’re preparing for exams, catching up on missed lectures, or simply looking for better ways to study, NoteStash is here to help. We provide a collaborative space for students to upload, organize, and find course-specific notes easily and efficiently. Our mission is to make studying smarter, not harder, by fostering a community where knowledge is shared. NoteStash is the go-to resource for university students seeking academic support at their fingertips.
